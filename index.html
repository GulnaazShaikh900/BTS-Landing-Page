BTS Concert

South Korean boy band BTS has performed in five concert tours (three of which have been worldwide), six fan meeting tours, four joint tours, eight showcases, and 41 concerts since their debut in 2013. BTS' debut solo concert tour in 2014, The Red Bullet Tour, began in Asia and then expanded to Australia, North America, and South America, attracting 80,000 spectators. In the midst of The Red Bullet Tour, BTS also held its first concert tour of Japan, Wake Up: Open Your Eyes Japan Tour. In 2015, BTS commenced The Most Beautiful Moment in Life On Stage Tour.

SOFI STADIUM

Los Angeles: On November 29, Christy Castillo Butcher, SoFi Stadium's Senior VP of Programming revealed that BTS sold out four shows for the first time in SoFi Stadium history. This concert achieved the highest ticket sales among any concert held by a single band or artist at the SoFi Stadium.

About

Our fanbase started on April 28th, 2014 with just one admin and kicked off as BTS West Coast ARMY, the first fanbase to solely represent ARMYs in the United States. On March 17th, 2015, we expanded to become US BTS ARMY. Over the years as the fandom and BTS’ popularity grew in the US, we officially became a nonprofit organization in the State of California in February 2019. With these new changes, the future looks bright and promising as we take one more step forward to support BTS and ARMY alike.
